About Pokemon Fan-Vote

Welcome to Pokemon Fan-Vote,this website is created by Aarush Sharma , a 17 y/o High School Student for the project Waffles. So basically users can come here and see the different pokemons from the list and select their favourite one and then vote for them :D , made by a hardcore pokemon fan.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the voting system work? +

Simply select your favorite Pokemon from the list and tell us why you love them.

Are all Pokemon generations included? +

Yes! I tried to include as many pokemons as i could and from different generations.

Can I vote multiple times? +

Yes you can spam the votes , vote however many times you like.

Inspiration behind this? +

I am very big pokemon fan as well as card collector , and i have a knack for waffles hence this project came into being. Check #waffles on slack.
